## Description (4000 chars max)

```
Plan epic motorcycle tours across Britain with VisorUp — the free touring companion built by riders, for riders.

ROUTE BUILDER
Build custom routes with drag-and-drop waypoints, road preference controls (fast, curvy, or twisty), and automatic fuel stops every 100 miles from 9,913 UK fuel stations. Add points of interest, plan multi-day trips, and export GPX files to share with friends.

LIVE RIDE MODE
Hit the road with GPS tracking, turn-by-turn navigation, speed and ETA display, and automatic rerouting if you go off course. Toggle voice navigation on or off, and track your ride stats in real time.

EXPLORE 1,889 POINTS OF INTEREST
Curated motorcycle-friendly stops across Britain — cafes, viewpoints, passes, castles, and fuel stations. Tap any POI to see details and add it to your route with one tap.

PLAN YOUR TRIP
Check weather forecasts along your route, calculate fuel costs, and use the interactive packing checklist so you never forget your disc lock again.

COMMUNITY
Share ride photos, post updates, and see what other riders are up to. Like and comment on posts from the VisorUp community.

ROUTE SHARING
Export any route as a GPX file and share it via WhatsApp, email, or AirDrop. Perfect for group rides.

FEATURES AT A GLANCE
• Custom route builder with road preferences
• Live GPS ride mode with turn-by-turn
• 9,913 UK fuel stations with auto-stop planning
• 1,889 curated points of interest
• Weather overlay for route planning
• Multi-day trip planning with day breaks
• Voice navigation (toggleable)
• GPX export and route sharing
• Community feed with photo uploads
• Offline route caching
• Packing checklist
• Dark mode throughout

VisorUp is completely free with no ads. Built in Britain for British roads.

Questions or feedback? Email us at jack@visorup.co.uk
```

### Purposes

- **Location**: Used for live ride GPS tracking and showing nearby POIs. Only collected during active use.
- **Photos**: Users can optionally upload ride photos to the community feed.
- **Contact Info (email, name)**: Used for account creation and login only.
- **Analytics**: Aggregated usage data to improve the app. No third-party advertising.
- **Crash Data**: Collected to fix bugs and improve stability.

### Data NOT Collected
- Financial information
- Health or fitness data
- Browsing history
- Search history
- Contacts
- Diagnostics beyond crash/performance
